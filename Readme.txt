Modern Armor mod for Hearts of Iron 4

This mod aims to make medium tank battalions capable of upgrading to modern armor once the technology is researched.

#changelog
v1.0.1
	-Updated for Waking the Tiger
v1.0.0
	-initial release
	-Modern tanks have become medium tanks and will fill out medium tank battalions
	-Modern tank battalions have been removed from the game
	-Modern tank research adjusts the medium tank battalions so that they are identical to the old modern tank battalions
